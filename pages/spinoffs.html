Bloons TD Battles was released on December 12, 2012, The gameplay is similar to that in Bloons TD 5 but two players compete against one another in one of four game modes.

Like Bloons TD Battles, the core gameplay is similar to that of BTD5, however, the player has to maintain a city, expanding the city by capturing bloon-infested areas called tiles

Bloons Adventure Time TD is a crossover game between Ninja Kiwi's Bloons TD series and Cartoon Network's Adventure Time series, it was announced via the Cartoon Network International News Site and the Ninja Kiwi blog on March 8 and 9, 2018, respectively.

Bloons TD Battles 2, the sequel to Bloons TD Battles, was released on November 30, 2021.[67] It was first leaked in Ninja Kiwi's YouTube video for Bloons TD 6's 24.0 update
