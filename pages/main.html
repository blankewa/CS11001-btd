About Balloons Tower Defence

What is Bloons Tower Defense?

Bloons Tower Defense is a series of tower defense games developed by Ninja Kiwi. The objective of the game is to prevent bloons (balloons) from reaching the end of a path by placing various types of towers that pop the bloons.

History

The first game in the series, Bloons Tower Defense, was released in 2007. Since then, several sequels and spin-offs have been released, including Bloons TD 2, Bloons TD 3, and Bloons TD 4.
